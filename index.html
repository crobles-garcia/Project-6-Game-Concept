❁ WATER QUEST
The Journey to Clean Water
Inspired by charity: water
Welcome to Water Quest! Catch falling water with your bucket.
Time: 30 | Score: 0
How to Play:
  • Press "Start" to begin the game
  • Use the Left (←) and Right (→) arrow keys to move the bucket
  • Catch falling water drops to gain points
  • You have 30 seconds to collect as much water as you can!
